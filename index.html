About Me

Since I was young, I have always been inspired by the world around me. For the past 9 years, video editing and digital design has been the main way I express my love for the things I'm passionate about. Looking at my past work, I see that I'm ultimately driven by my unique perspective on relating to the world around me. No matter the medium, I enjoy inspiring the creativity in others around me.


When I'm not on the ice playing hockey, it is my passion to design, edit, code and create. I am currently pursuring my niche for coding through SMU's coding bootcamp. I have a focus on front end development and am excited to see what is next on my journey.
